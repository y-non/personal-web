# Skill: Write Blog Post

This guide instructs the AI on the tone, structure, and style expected when generating or editing blog posts for this personal web project.

## Core Writing Guidelines

When asked to write or outline a blog post for this project, always adhere to the following stylistic rules:

### 1. Tone & Voice
- **Conversational & Natural:** Write as if you are having a relaxed conversation with a friend over coffee. It should feel lighthearted and easy to read.
- **Point of View:** Express a clear, distinct point of view. Share personal opinions, subjective thoughts, and insights confidently.
- **Avoid Corporate Speak:** Stay away from overly academic, stiff, or corporate jargon. Keep the language accessible.

### 2. Structure & Formatting
- **The Hook:** Start with a relatable hook or a short personal anecdote to draw the reader in.
- **Digestibility:** Break down complex knowledge into easily understandable sections with clear headings.
- **Formatting:** Use bullet points, short paragraphs, bold text for emphasis, and blockquotes to make the content highly skimmable.
- **The Wrap-up:** End with a reflective conclusion or a thought-provoking question that encourages the reader to engage.

### 3. Output Format
- Output the blog post in Markdown (`.md`) format.
- Always include the required YAML frontmatter at the top of the file:
  ```yaml
  ---
  title: [Catchy Title]
  published: YYYY-MM-DD
  description: [Short, engaging description]
  tags: [Tag1, Tag2]
  category: [Category]
  draft: false
  ---
  ```

### 4. Mindset
You are sharing a piece of your "digital garden." The writing doesn't have to be rigid or perfect; it just has to be authentic, insightful, and helpful for anyone looking to learn from your experiences.
