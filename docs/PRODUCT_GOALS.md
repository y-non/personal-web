# Product Goals

## Short-term Goals
- Establish a seamless writing workflow for new blog posts.
- Ensure the site is fully responsive and accessible.
- Personalize the Fuwari template to match the author's brand and aesthetic.

## Long-term Goals
- Build a consistent audience through regular, high-quality knowledge sharing.
- Integrate advanced features like a Table of Contents (TOC) for long-form articles.
- Maintain a perfect Lighthouse score for performance, SEO, and accessibility.
- Create an engaging reading experience with smooth transitions and typography.
